FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of crochet designs do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of crochet designs for sarees, shawls, scarves, and other accessories. Our collection is inspired by traditional and modern crochet techniques to bring you unique, high-quality designs.

2. Can I customize a crochet design for my saree?

Yes! We offer customization services where you can choose the colors, patterns, and even the type of yarn for your crochet design. Contact us with your requirements, and we’ll create something special just for you!

3. What materials are used in the crochet designs?

We use only the highest quality yarns such as cotton, silk, and wool. The material used depends on the design and the look you're going for. Each design is carefully made with durability and comfort in mind.

4. How long does it take to complete a crochet design?

The time required to complete a design varies depending on the complexity and size of the project. Generally, it takes anywhere from 1 to 3 weeks. For customized designs, it may take a bit longer. We’ll always keep you updated on the progress!

5. How should I care for my crochet saree or accessory?

To ensure your crochet designs last, gently hand wash them in cold water using mild detergent. Avoid wringing the fabric, and lay it flat to dry. For best results, store your crochet items in a cool, dry place to prevent stretching or damage.

6. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary depending on your location. Please contact us for more details.

7. How can I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website. Browse our designs and choose your favorite crochet piece. If you want a custom order, you can fill out the form on our customization page and we will reach out to you with more details.

8. Do you offer returns or exchanges?

Due to the handmade nature of our crochet designs, we do not accept returns or exchanges unless there is a defect in the product. Please ensure your order is correct before placing it, and contact us if there’s an issue with your item.
